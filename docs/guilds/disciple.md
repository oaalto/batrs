# Disciple

Chaos spawn cues, colored combat lines, and **`@wearall`** after the chaotic spawn transform line.

## Line highlights

- Chaos **slowing down** line: red, with a loud banner so you notice spawn dropping.
- Other red/green highlights for exhaustion, fumbles, chaos pulse, tentacle hits, frenzy.

## Shortcuts

### Skills

| Shortcut | Full name | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| `ucs` | Chaotic spawn | Optional target: bare `use 'chaotic spawn'` or `use 'chaotic spawn' <args>`. |
| `uc` | Clawed strike | Standard Batrs `use` targeting (optional target). |
| `ukd` | Kiss of death | Target required (`target …;use 'kiss of death' at …`). |

## Profile

Enable **`disciple`** in **`/guilds`**.
