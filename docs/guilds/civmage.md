# Civmage

Floating disc helpers (cast, get/put), identify, lift of load, mirror image, spam heal, plus a few safety highlights for odd feelings and a wobbling disc.

## Line highlights

- Odd “you feel odd…” line in red.
- If the disc wavers, a bold **FLOATING DISC IS GOING DOWN!** banner is added.
- Standard **magic lore** coloring on analyse-style lines.

## Shortcuts

### Spells

| Shortcut | Full name | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| `cld` | Lift of load | No args: `cast lift of load at me`. With args: at target. |
| `cid` | Identify | No args: `cast identify at me`. With args: at target. |
| `cfd` | Floating disc | Uses Batrs floating-disc helper line. |
| `chf` | Heal self (repeat) | `repeat inf cast heal self`. |
| `cmi` | Mirror image | No args: `cast mirror image at me`. With args: at target. |

### Other

| Shortcut | Full name | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| `gad` | Get all from disc | `get all from my disc` helper. |
| `gaad` | Get all armour from disc | Inventory helper. |
| `gawd` | Get all weapon from disc | Inventory helper. |
| `pd` | Put noeq in disc | `put noeq in my disc` helper. |

## Profile

Enable **`civmage`** in **`/guilds`**.
