# Psionicist

Mental defence progress in tiered colors, stunned intrusion lines, auto **psi sense** on some kills, and a full psionic cast list.

## Line highlights

Training messages for mental defence are colored by urgency. Certain kill lines trigger **psi sense** and blue emphasis.

## Shortcuts

### Skills

| Shortcut | Full name | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| `med` | Meditation | `use meditation` (unquoted). |
