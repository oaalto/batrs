# Sabres

Lounging, sabre fence, glove knock, and re-wield — with highlights for cadence and fence outcomes. Needs your **main-hand weapon** on file.

## Line highlights

Lounging mood lines (yellow/green), **battle cadence** (blue), fence hit/miss coloring, auto-wield after glove-knock lines, green on successful wield.

## Shortcuts

### Skills

| Shortcut | Full name | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| `ul` | Lounging | Standard `use` targeting. |
| `usf` | Sabre fence | No args: `use 'sabre fence'`. With target: `target …; use sabre fence at …` (unquoted mid-form). |
| `ug` | Gloveknock | Needs **`sabre_weapon`** and target: `remove <weapon> from right hand`; `target`; `use gloveknock at …`. |

### Other

| Shortcut | Full name | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| `wsw` | Wield sabre | `wield <sabre_weapon>` from profile/settings. |

## Profile

Set **`sabre_weapon`** (also in **`/guilds`**). Enable **`sabres`**.
